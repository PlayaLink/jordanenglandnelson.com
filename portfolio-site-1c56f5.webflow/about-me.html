I'm a trilingual product designer with technical chops, a background in journalism, and a passion for distilling complexity into clarity.
I approach product design like a reporter sniffing out a story: observing behaviors and trends, tracking down the right people and information, and focusing on the larger context so that my solution addresses the problem, instead of just satisfying the ask.
I live for those “a-ha!” research moments – translating them into impactful stories and product innovations that make a difference in a person’s day.
